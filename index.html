Data Analysis Project for Predicting GPA and PC Possession

Key Words: SAS, GPA predict, PC possession rate, Logistic model

  • Using a linear model to fit the GPA prediction model, we use two common methods to select variables, one is to select them according to the p-value (t-test), the other one is to apply a stepwise selection method.
  • Building a logistic model to predict PC possession rate, also, use the p-value selection method and stepwise selection method to delete insignificant variables.
  • Do model diagnostics for all models, detect outliers, and interpret them by analyzing their detailed information.

Survey of Sleeping Quality for SUSTech Students

Key Words: Sampling survey, Stratified sampling, ANOVA test, Comparison

  • Conducted an entire sample survey process (include focus group meeting, questionnaire design, pilot study, sample size calculation, data collection & analysis, and presentation, etc.) to get an overview of the sleeping quality of SUSTech students.
  • Compared the results with the nationwide average case from the previous study and mainly use the ANOVA test to detect the difference between SUSTech students and average case.
